website redesign

The main goal of this rebrand was to strengthen the brand identity and ensure consistency across all platforms, including their restaurant, packaging, and social media. I redesigned their website to align with their packaging design and simplified the user interface for a more cohesive experience.

clothing

These pieces were made from second-hand materials, such as sweaters and tablecloths. Each piece represents a different character from the fable The Snow Queen and takes a unique approach to storytelling. The Snow Queen was a misunderstood character in a patriarchal environment, so for this project, I aimed to highlight the feminine side of each character.
